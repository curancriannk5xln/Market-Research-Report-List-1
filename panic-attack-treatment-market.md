<p><p>The panic attack treatment market comprises several key drug categories: Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitors (SSRIs), Serotonin Norepinephrine Reuptake Inhibitors (SNRIs), and Benzodiazepines (BZDs). SSRIs and SNRIs are primarily used for long-term management, enhancing serotonin and norepinephrine levels to reduce anxiety. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to different patient needs and treatment timelines in managing panic disorders.</p></p>
