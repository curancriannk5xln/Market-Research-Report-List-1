<p><p>The coconut oil market comprises two primary types: Fresh Coconut and Dry Copra. Fresh Coconut involves the extraction of oil from freshly harvested coconuts, offering a high-quality product with vibrant flavor and nutritional benefits. In contrast, the Dry Copra market utilizes dried coconut meat, which is more stable and easier to transport, making it a cost-effective option for producing oil. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with Fresh Coconut appealing to health-conscious buyers and Dry Copra appealing to bulk processors.</p></p>
